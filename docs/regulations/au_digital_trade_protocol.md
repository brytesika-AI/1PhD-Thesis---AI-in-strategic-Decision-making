# AU Protocol on Digital Trade - Strategic Summary

## Key Objectives
The Protocol aims to create a harmonized framework for digital trade across the African continent, ensuring data sovereignty and promoting local digital development.

## Strategic Constraints for SA Corporations
1. **Cross-Border Data Flows**: While promoting trade, the protocol respects national security and data protection requirements.
2. **Algorithmic Transparency**: Encourages transparency in algorithmic systems to prevent anti-competitive behavior.
3. **Digital Sovereignty**: Firms are encouraged to maintain control over their critical digital assets and intellectual property.

## Contextual Implication
The AI-SRF ensures "Algorithmic Sovereignty" (ASY) by grounding models in local institutional memory rather than adopting foreign-market assumptions.
